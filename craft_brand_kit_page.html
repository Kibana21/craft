Compositing rules
· Logo is never generated by AI — always composited from this vault
· Clear space is enforced programmatically — no text or imagery enters the padding ring
· Primary logo is used when the poster has ≥ 180px width available in the logo zone
· Secondary icon mark is used for WhatsApp cards and narrow zones
· SVG format ensures pixel-perfect rendering at any export resolution

· One kit is active at a time across the entire organisation
· Restoring a past version immediately applies it to all new projects and artifact generations
· Existing published artifacts retain the kit version they were generated under
· Brand Admins are notified by email when a kit is activated or restored
